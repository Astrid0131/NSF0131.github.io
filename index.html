NSF Workshop Introduction



The NSF Workshop on the Implication of Urban Scale Occupant Behavior for Resilient Building Design, Operation, and Policy Making is a groundbreaking event designed to catalyze interdisciplinary dialogue and innovation at the intersection of urban planning, behavioral science, and resilience studies. This workshop aims to bring together a diverse group of leading academics, industry experts, and policy makers to explore the critical role of occupant behavior in shaping the resilience of urban buildings and communities. By focusing on the dynamic interactions between people and the built environment, the event seeks to uncover new insights and strategies for creating more sustainable, adaptable, and occupant-friendly urban spaces. With a roster of esteemed participants and a commitment to fostering collaborative solutions, this workshop promises to be a pivotal moment for advancing our understanding and implementation of resilient urban design and policy.
